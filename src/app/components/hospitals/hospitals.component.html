Which are the best hospitals in the UAE?
In 2000, the country had only three hospitals in the UAE that had international accreditation. By 2016, there were 55 hospitals throughout the country that had the JCI accreditation. Most of these hospitals have introduced best practices and patented medicines that are approved by the USFDA or better known as the US Food and Drug Administration. This accreditation has enabled the availability of highest quality care and medicines with patents drawn from across the world. The country is well equipped to deal with millions coming to its shores for better treatment facilities and proper care. With the public and private sector working closely to build more hospitals and recruit highly qualified foreign doctors and medical staff, the quality of healthcare is going to rise more significantly.
